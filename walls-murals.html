Direct-to-Wall Technology

No Wrap.
No Liner.
Just Ink.

Traditional wallpaper and vinyl wraps are installed on top of your surface — which means seams, bubbles, and an eventual peel. Our UV flatbed printer bonds ink directly into the wall surface at a molecular level. The result is seamless, vibrant, and permanent.

◆

Photo-Realistic Resolution

Millions of colours, razor-sharp detail. We print at gallery-quality resolution across any wall size.

◆

Works on Textured Surfaces

Drywall with orange peel texture? Wood grain panelling? Most interior surfaces are printable.

◆

No Adhesive, No Residue

If you ever repaint, you’re painting over ink — not removing layers of sticky backing.
